Snippets

Type a keyword in any app and it expands to the text you define. For example, typing !email becomes your email address. macOS only.

Text expansion is not available on this platform or build.

Text expansion needs Accessibility and Input Monitoring permission to detect typing and paste replacements. Grant permission, then enable the feature.

When enabled, snippets expand as you type in other apps. Disabled by default for privacy.
